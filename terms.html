About the App

Cognito is an AI-powered flashcard application for iOS that helps users create, study, and retain knowledge using spaced repetition. The App includes:

  • Flashcard creation (manual and AI-generated from text and images)
  • Spaced repetition study powered by the FSRS algorithm
  • Multiple study modes: Classic, Compact, Quiz, and Type Answer
  • AI Tutor for explanations after incorrect answers
  • Diagram-to-cards conversion from labeled images
  • Analytics dashboard with heatmaps, retention trends, and weak topic analysis
  • Import from Anki (.apkg) and CSV files
