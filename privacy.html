Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 02, 2025

Cognito respects your privacy. This app does not collect, track, or share any personal information. All flashcards and progress are stored securely on your device or in your iCloud account (if enabled).

Data Collection

Cognito does not collect any user data. No analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking systems are used.

Accounts and Sync

No registration or login is required to use Cognito. Your data is stored locally and optionally synchronized through your iCloud account using Apple’s secure infrastructure.

Purchases and Subscriptions

AI-powered features are available via optional subscription. All purchases are handled securely using Apple’s StoreKit. We do not store or access any payment information.

Third-Party Libraries

Cognito uses only official Apple frameworks provided by iOS/macOS. No third-party libraries are included.

Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. Since no information is collected or stored by us, there is nothing to delete or export on our end.
